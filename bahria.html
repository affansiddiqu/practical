BAHRIA TOWN

ABOUT

“Rome wasn’t built in a day” can only be believed by someone who hasn’t witnessed the development speed of Bahria Town Karachi project. The sheer scale, logistics and quality of this world-class project is beyond anything experienced by Pakistan before. Launched in 2014, in only 8 years it has already become a “city within a city” which is a living community with thousands of residents, enjoying an enviable lifestyle not available anywhere else in Pakistan. Designed with a neighborhood concept to provide outstanding facilities to its residents, it has introduced new benchmarks of community development. And the attention to details ensure that all imaginable amenities and services are available which were unimaginable in Pakistan. Image result for about bahria town karachi Bahria Town Lahore is probably the most developed chapter of Bahria Town that was designed and developed on modern standards. It has the highest level of infrastructure that adds value to the property as well as the living standard of the people Bahria Town Karachi is a privately owned gated suburb just off the M-9 Motorway northeast of Karachi, Pakistan. The suburb is being developed by the Bahria Town Group, and occupies over 46,000 acres (72 sq mi; 19,000 ha). Construction started in 2014 and will continue.
